Case600FF Model QSS Notes

The Dymola FMU was used for QSS simulations.

The key room temperature variables, TRooQSS_K, outputs are the primary
basis for solution comparison.

Command lines for a 63,072 s output sampling rate:
QSS --qss=QSS1 --dtOut=63072 --out=sfx Case600FF.fmu
QSS --qss=QSS2 --dtOut=63072 --out=sfx Case600FF.fmu
QSS --qss=LIQSS1 --dtInf=100 --dtOut=63072 --out=sfx Case600FF.fmu
QSS --qss=LIQSS2 --dtInf=100 --dtOut=63072 --out=sfx Case600FF.fmu
