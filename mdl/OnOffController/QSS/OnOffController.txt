OnOffController Model QSS Notes

The QSS runs used the Dymola FMU (the available JModelica FMU depends on
libgfortran.so3 and so cannot be run on Fedora 27 at Objexx and
compile_fmu.JModelica.py is failing with the .mo).

The QSS runs use "all" outputs to get accurate view of the temperature:
QSS OnOffController.fmu --qss=<QSS_method> --out=rafx

Notes
 Need "all" or "observer" outputs to show the temperature accurately
