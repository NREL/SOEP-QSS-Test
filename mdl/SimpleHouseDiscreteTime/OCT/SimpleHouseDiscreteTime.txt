SimpleHouseDiscreteTime Model OCT Notes

This model no longer builds with the current OCT due to removal of some of its dependencies in the Buildings library.

OCT generated FMUs with event indicator support do not currently run under PyFMI
(they get stuck in an infinite FMI initialization loop) so we use separate FMUs
for PyFMI and QSS simulation.
