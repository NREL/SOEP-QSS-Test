EventIndicator4 Model QSS Notes

QSS is not sensitive to the numerical differentiation time step or the
zero-crossing options in this case where the zero-crossing functions
actually cross zero.

The discrete variable is currently being tracked as a QSS variable.
Since it is an output variable in this model it gets updated by QSS
due to the dependencies in the Outputs section of the XML. But
variables that will be short-circuited (discrete, integer, boolean, ...)
should not be tracked by QSS. Once the short-circuiting design is
finalized the necessary changes will be made in QSS.
